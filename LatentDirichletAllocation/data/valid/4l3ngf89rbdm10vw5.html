IDC 2013 Conference

Thanks to all participants, organizers and volunteers for being a part of a very exciting and memorable conference. All session videos are now posted on the IDC 2013 Video Archive. Download the Conference Program and access papers published in the ACM Digital Library. You may still purchase IDC T-shirts. Stay tuned for IDC 2014 in Aarhus, Denmark, June 17-20, 2014.

Remembering Amy Koumis: Amy was a talented young woman whose warmth, creativity and imagination was vibrantly felt by all who met her during her time as a volunteer and participant at IDC 2013. Not long after IDC, Amy tragically passed away on August 21st, just shy of her 24th birthday. Learn more about Amy Koumis and share your thoughts

The 2013 Conference in NYC

IDC is the premiere annual international conference for the top minds in academia and industry to share the latest research findings, innovative methodologies and new technologies in the areas of inclusive child-centered design, learning and interaction. Participants often come from a variety of fields, including computer science, communication, child development, engineering, digital media, game design, educational psychology and learning sciences. The conference incorporates panels, workshops and exhibits organized around papers, presentations, speakers, and discussions.

This year’s conference offered a special emphasis on supporting DIY/maker culture among children and global inclusion, particularly for marginalized children worldwide. Learn More >>
