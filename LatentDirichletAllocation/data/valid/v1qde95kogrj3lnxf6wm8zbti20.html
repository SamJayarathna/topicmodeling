MobiSys 2009

The 7th Annual International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services,
June 22-25, 2009 in Kraków, Poland. 

MobiSys 2009 seeks to present innovative and significant research on the design, implementation, usage, and evaluation of mobile computing and wireless systems, applications, and services. This conference builds on the success of the previous six MobiSys conferences. It is jointly sponsored by ACM SIGMOBILE and the USENIX Association.
