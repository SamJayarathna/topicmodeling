The 10th ACM SIGPLAN International Conference on Functional Programming (ICFP 2005)

Tallinn, Estonia
September 26-28, 2005
(local information)
(programme)

Co-located with TFP 2005 (September 23-24) and GPCE 2005 (September 29 - October 1).

ICFP 2005 provides a forum for researchers and developers to hear about the latest work on the design, implementations, principles, and uses of functional programming. The conference covers the entire spectrum of work, from practice to theory, including its peripheries.

Deadlines

  • Submission: April 13, 2005.
  • On-line response to reviews: May 19-21, 2005.
  • Author notification: June 3, 2005.
  • Camera-ready copy: July 10, 2005.

ICFP 2005 is co-located with TFP 2005 and GPCE 2005, and features the following workshops:

  • Saturday 24: The 2005 ACM SIGPLAN Workshop on Scheme and Functional Programming.
  • Saturday 24: Commercial Users of Functional Programming.
  • Sunday 25: The Fourth ACM SIGPLAN Erlang Workshop.
  • Sunday 25: Functional and Declarative Programming in Education (FDPE05).
  • Thursday 29: The 2005 ACM SIGPLAN Workshop on ML.
  • Thursday 29: Workshop on Curry and Functional Logic Programming (WCFLP 2005).
  • Friday 30: The 2005 Haskell Workshop.
  • Friday 30: Workshop on MEchanized Reasoning about Languages with varIable biNding (MERλIN 2005).
