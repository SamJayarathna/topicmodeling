International Conference on Distributed Event-Based Systems

Welcome to DEBS.org

This site provides information about upcoming and previous Distributed Event-Based Systems (DEBS) conferences and workshops. It also con tains papers from the DEBS PhD workshops.

Following the success of the inaugural DEBS 2007 conference in Toronto, the Second DEBS 2008 conference was held in Rome, Italy. The Third DEBS 2009 took place at Vanderbilt University Campus, Nashville, TN, USA. The Fourth DEBS 2010 will be in Cambridge, United Kingdom.
