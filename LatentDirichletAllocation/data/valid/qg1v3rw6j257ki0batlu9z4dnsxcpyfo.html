Rio Othon Palace, Copacabana, Rio de Janeiro,September 3 to 6, 2007

SBCCi is an international forum dedicated to integrated circuits and systems design,
test and CAD, held anualy in Brazil. The 20th SBCCI will take place at Rio de Janeiro,
Brazil. The goal of the symposium is to bring together researchers in the areas of
computer-aided design, design and test of integrated circuits and systems. The scope
of the symposium includes technical sessions, tutorials and panels, as well as
exhibition and working group meetings. A special issue of the the Analog
Integrated Circuits and Signal Processing journal by Springer will be published
with extended versions of the selected analog/RF/mixed signal papers presented at
the symposium. In addition, the authors of the best papers will be invited to submit
extended versions to IEEE Design Test of Computers.
