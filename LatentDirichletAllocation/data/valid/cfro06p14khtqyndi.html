DocEng 2011

Join us at the Googleplex for the 11th ACM Symposium on Document Engineering.

DocEng is an annual conference dedicated to all aspects of Document Engineering.  The Call for Papers describes the topics of interest at DocEng, but papers presented at previous DocEng conferences have covered topics ranging from page layout algorithms to display hardware.

We are please to announce that Dr John Warnock (co-found of Adobe Systems) and Dr Mark Davis (Google, President of the Unicode Consortium) are the Keynote Speakers for DocEng 2011.

A series of workshops will be held on the first day of the conference, Monday 19 September 2011.  These will be followed by the three-day symposium on Tuesday 20 - Thursday 22 September 2011.  Google is hosting the event at its headquarters in Mountain View California, a short distance from San Francisco.

You can also join our public discussion newsgroup.

What's New:

DocEng 2011 is now complete!  Thank you to everyone who attended.

Two-thirds of the talks were recorded and can be viewed on YouTube.  See the conference program for links.
