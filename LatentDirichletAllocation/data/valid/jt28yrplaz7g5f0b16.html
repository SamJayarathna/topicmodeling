CASCON 2014 Register Now!

"Smarter Systems of Interactions"


November 3rd - November 5th, 2014

The confluence of technologies such as mobile platforms, social computing, cloud computing, big data, Internet of things, analytics and others are bringing transformational paradigm shifts in industry and society. The purposeful interaction, integration, and interoperation of these technologies together will create novel, high-value business capabilities, services, and solutions in healthcare, banking, commerce, cities and many other industry domains.

CASCON 2014 Program Co-Chairs

The co-chairs for CASCON 2014 will be :

  • Jin Li, User Experience Design Lead, IBM Canada Software Lab
  • Ken Wong, Associate Professor, Department of Computing Science, University of Alberta

Emerging Technologies Track (ETT)

Emerging technologies such as low-cost sensing, wireless and mobile devices, social and cloud computing platforms, and big data analytics are fundamentally transforming the information technology industry. The confluence of these technologies offers amazing opportunities for advancement, innovation, and paradigm shifts, creating new and high-impact solutions for critical, real-world problems in numerous industrial domains, such as commerce, finance, energy, and health care. We invite position papers to share visions, ideas, and insights on these advancements, highlight technology gaps, research agendas, and integrated solutions, and initiate discussion, feedback, and further collaboration.

Call for Position Papers, Important Dates :

  • Notification September 19th, 2014
  • Camera-ready position papers Due October 3rd, 2014

Workshops

CASCON 2014 workshops are scheduled on the second and third day, Both presentation style workshops as well as hands-on workshops are available, and are run as either half day (3 hour) or full day (6 hour) sessions. Hands-on workshops are particularly popular and filled up quickly when registration is opened. Take note on the open date for workshop registration.

Technology Showcases

CASCON 2014 will feature more than 50 showcases from IBM, academia and industry partners to demonstrate their latest development of advanced software technologies. The show cases will be on display throughout the conference and the presenters will have the opportunity to earn two awards: Best Technology Showcase Award selected from a selection committee and People's Choice Award for the showcase which receives the highest votes from viewers
