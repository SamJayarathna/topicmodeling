ACM Symposium on Virtual Reality Software and Technology

November 7 - 9, 2005
Naval Postgraduate School, Monterey, California

  
 
 

Contact : Gurminder Singh

  
 
 

ACM Virtual Reality Software and Technology (VRST) is an annual conference devoted to the technical aspects of virtual reality. The first VRST was held in Singapore in 1994 and since then it has been held in Japan, Hong Kong, Switzerland, Taiwan, England, Korea, and Canada. This will be the twelfth VRST annual conference to be held in Monterey, California, USA.
