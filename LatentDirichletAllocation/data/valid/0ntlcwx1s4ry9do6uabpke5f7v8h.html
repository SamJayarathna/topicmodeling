General Information Important Dates
MobiSys 2006 will take place in Uppsala, Sweden in the middle of June. Uppsala is an old town which is dominated by its University - the oldest in Scandinavia with about 38,000 students. Getting there is easy - 35 minutes by train from Stockholm or 35 minutes by bus from Stockholm-Arlanda International Airport.

The conference is scheduled to end the day before the Midsummer festivities in Sweden. Don't miss the opportunity to extend your stay and experience an old Swedish tradition.

NEW! Prof. Daniel P Siewiorek has been awarded SIGMOBILE's 2006 Outstanding Contributions Award "for pioneering and fundamental contributions to wearable and context-aware computing". The award will be announced and presented on Tuesday, June 20, 2006 during the opening ceremony.
