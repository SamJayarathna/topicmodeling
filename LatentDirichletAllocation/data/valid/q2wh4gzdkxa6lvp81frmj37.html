Thank you for joining us in Seattle for MobiSys 2005. Online proceedings are now available to USENIX members on the USENIX Web site and to ACM Digital Library subscribers in the ACM Digital Library.

Workshops
MobiSys 2005 was co-located with two workshops for highly focused discussions of new and emerging topics:

  • EESR '05: Workshop on End-to-End, Sense-and-Respond Systems, Applications, and Services
  • WiTMeMo '05: International Workshop on Wireless Traffic Measurements and Modeling
Both workshops took place Sunday, June 5, 2005.
