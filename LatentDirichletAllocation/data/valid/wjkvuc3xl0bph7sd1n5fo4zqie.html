Network and Operating System Support for Digital Audio and Video (NOSSDAV)

ACM NOSSDAV is a uniquely interactive and discussion-oriented workshop attended by top researchers, students, developers, and practitioners from academia and industry to discuss new ideas and future directions in multimedia applications, networking, operating systems, and other related areas of computing. Over the years, the scope of ACM NOSSDAV has broadened to include networked games, sensor networks, multimedia interfaces, and peer-to-peer networking.

NOSSDAV is a conference/workshop taking place annually in early summer. See the following for information on hosting a future instance of NOSSDAV.

NOSSDAV is guided by a steering committee.
