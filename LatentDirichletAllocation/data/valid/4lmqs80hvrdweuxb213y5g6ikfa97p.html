WNS3 2014

The Workshop on ns-3 (WNS3) is a one day workshop to be held on May 7, 2014, on the campus of the Georgia Institute of Technology in Atlanta GA. The objective of the workshop is to gather ns-3 users and developers, together with networking simulation practitioners and users, and developers of other network simulation tools, to discuss the ns-3 simulator and related activities.
