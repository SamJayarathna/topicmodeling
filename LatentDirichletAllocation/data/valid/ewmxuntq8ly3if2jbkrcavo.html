30th Spring conference on Computer Graphics

This event is probably the oldest regular annual meeting of Computer Graphics community in Central Europe.

The Spring Conference on Computer Graphics attempts to cover all interesting projects from computer graphics, image processing and applications. The philosophy of SCCG is to put together top experts with young researchers in CG and to support a good communication channel for East-West exchange of prospective ideas.

Date and venue

The conference will be held in Smolenice castle, Slovakia (about 120 km near Vienna).

Date: May 28-30, 2014

Conference chair: prof. Diego Gutierrez
Zaragoza
  
Invited speakers: Kavita Bala
Cornell University
  
Thabo Beeler
Disney Research Zürich
  
Matthias Zwicker
University of Bern

Registration and fees

The conference registration will open on April 22, 2014. The registration also involves booking of accommodation and related services (meals, transport) which are not part of the conference fee.

The conference fee is 320,- EUR (350,- EUR cash on site). The fee includes access to all the conference sessions, printed proceedings and refreshments inbetween the sessions.
