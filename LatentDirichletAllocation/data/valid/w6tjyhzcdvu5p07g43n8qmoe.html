    The 7th ACM WSDM Conference (WSDM2014) will take place in Crowne Plaza Times Square, New York City, during February 24-28, 2014.

    WSDM 2014 is a highly selective, single track meeting that will include invited talks as well as oral and poster presentations of refereed papers. WSDM 2014 will also include workshops and tutorials in the related research areas.

    • Best Student Paper Award Dmitry Lagun, Mikhail Ageev, Qi Guo and Eugene Agichtein: Discovering Common Motifs in Cursor Movement Data for Improving Web Search Ranking
