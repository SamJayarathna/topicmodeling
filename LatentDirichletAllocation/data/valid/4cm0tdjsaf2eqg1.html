The organizing committee is delighted to invite you to ACM SIGCOMM 2009, to be held in Spain, Barcelona between August 17-21, 2009. SIGCOMM is the flagship annual conference of the Special Interest Group on Data Communications, a special interest group of the Association for Computing Machinery.
