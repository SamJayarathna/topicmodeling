in Cooperation with ACM - SIGMM

The 8th International Conference on Advances in Mobile Computing & Multimedia (MoMM2010) is a leading international conference for researchers and industry practitioners to share their new ideas, original research results and practical development experiences from all mobile computing and multimedia related areas.

MoMM2010 is endorsed by the International Organization for Information Integration and Web-based Applications & Services (@WAS), organized by The University of Paris 8 and The University of Cergy-Pontoise, Paris (France) (France) and will be held from 8 - 10 November 2010, in Paris, France in conjunction with the 12th International Conference on Information Integration and Web-based Applications & Services (iiWAS2010) and the International Conference on Ambient Systems, Networks and Technologies (ANT-2010).
