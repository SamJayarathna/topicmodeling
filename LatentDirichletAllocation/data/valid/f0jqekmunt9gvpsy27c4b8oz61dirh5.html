The First Workshop on

Behavioural Modelling - Foundations and Application

BM-FA 2009

The first workshop on Behavioural Modelling in MDA (BM-MDA) organized by the same Organizing Committee took place in collaboration with ECMDA-FA in 2009 in Twente, the Netherlands.

More than 40 participants attended the workshop. 7 papers and the invited talk were presented. The Proceedings were published in the ACM International Conference Proceeding Series; Vol. 379 , 2009, ISBN: 978-1-60558-503-1.
