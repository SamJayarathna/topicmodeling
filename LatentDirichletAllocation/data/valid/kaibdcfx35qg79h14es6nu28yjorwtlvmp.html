MobiHoc 2004, the Fifth ACM International Symposium on Mobile Ad Hoc Networking and Computing, is the fifth of a series of annual meetings sponsored by ACM SIGMOBILE, focusing on the latest research in the rapidly growing area of mobile ad hoc networking and computing. MobiHoc 2004 will be held in Tokyo, Japan, on Monday through Wednesday, May 24-26, 2004.

The first MobiHoc was held in 2000 in Boston, Massachusetts, as a workshop affiliated with the MobiCom conference also sponsored by SIGMOBILE. Since then, MobiHoc has become a separate symposium, held in 2001 at Long Beach, California, in 2002 at Lausanne, Switzerland, in 2003 at Annapolis, Maryland, and now in 2004 at Tokyo, Japan.
