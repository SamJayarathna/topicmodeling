Twenty-Fifth Annual ACM SIGACT-SIGOPS Symposium on
Principles of Distributed Computing
(PODC 2006)

July 23-26, 2006, Denver, Colorado, USA

PODC is a conference that focuses on research in the theory, design, specification and implementation of distributed systems. PODC is sponsored by ACM, SIGACT, and SIGOPS.

Anniversary Celebrations

The 25th Anniversary Celebrations will feature several events:
  1. Invited talks by:
    • Tushar Chandra (Google Inc.),
    • Danny Dolev (Hebrew University of Jerusalem),
    • Maurice Herlihy (Brown University),
    • Jon Kleinberg (Cornell University),
    • Werner Vogels (CTO, Amazon).
  2. Special celebration of 25 years of PODC on Sunday, including prizes, etc., before the conference starts. Organized by Maurice Herlihy, Dahlia Malkhi, and Mark Tuttle.
  3. Festive reception accompanied by a poster session on Sunday night.
