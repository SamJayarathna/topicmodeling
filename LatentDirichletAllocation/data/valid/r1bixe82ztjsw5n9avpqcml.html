2014 ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing

November 3-5, 2014, Seattle, WA

The ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing 2014 is the fifth in a new series of symposia that bring together researchers, developers, users, and practitioners interested in cloud computing. Co-sponsored by the ACM Special Interest Groups on Management of Data (SIGMOD) and on Operating Systems (SIGOPS), SoCC 2014 will take place in Seattle, WA.
