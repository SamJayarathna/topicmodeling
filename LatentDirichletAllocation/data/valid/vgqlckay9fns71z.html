ESUG 2010: Barcelona

The 18th ESUG International Smalltalk Conference will be held in Barcelona, Spain, Saturday, September 11 to Friday, September 17th.

The weekend (11/12) will be camp Smalltalk, the main conference starts Monday, 13th.
