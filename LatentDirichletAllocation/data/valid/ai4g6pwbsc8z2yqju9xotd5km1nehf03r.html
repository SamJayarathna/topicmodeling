The 2010 ACM SIGCOMM organization and program committees are delighted to invite you to submit your original and innovative work to the first ever SIGCOMM in India. SIGCOMM 2010 is the annual conference of the ACM Special Interest Group on Data Communication (SIGCOMM) on the applications, technologies, architectures, and protocols for computer communication.

News

  • SIGCOMM 2010 concluded on September 3, 2010 after a successful conference and set of workshops, with 500 attendees.
  • September 9, 2010: Main conference presentation slides online.
  • September 3, 2010: HomeNets and VISA papers online.
  • August 31, 2010: Main conference papers, and Poster and Demo articles online.
  • August 30, 2010: APSys, Green Networking and MobiHeld papers online.
  • August 25, 2010: General Chairs’ Message, PC Chairs’ Message and Welcome Note (including weather alert) online

  Check out the SIGCOMM Facebook Group if you would like to share a room with a fellow attendee.
