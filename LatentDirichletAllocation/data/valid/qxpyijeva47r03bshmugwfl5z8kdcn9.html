International Academic Research Conference

ACM-BCS Visions of Computer Science 2010

13-16 April 2010, Edinburgh University

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) and BCS, The Chartered Institute for IT, were delighted to host the joint ACM-BCS 2010 "Visions of Computer Science" conference, which was held at the Informatics Forum, Edinburgh University, between April 13-16 2010.

Programme (PDF)
Conference Speakers
Conference Proceedings

Some of the best papers will appear in the ACM Digital Library and The Computer Journal, the archival research publication of the BCS.  

CPHC

This year the CPHC conference was part of  the major 'Visions' conference to support the development of Computing as a whole. The conference aim was to have an inspirational and formative role in the advancement for all of the diverse areas of Computing.

Grand Challenges in Computing Research

Delegates attending the Visions conference had the opportunity to influence what new Grand Challenges the computing community will be working on over the coming decades.
