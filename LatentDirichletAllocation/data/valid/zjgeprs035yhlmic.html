ACM Symposium on Computational Geometry 2000

June 12 - June 14, 2000
Hong Kong University of Science and Technology

Sponsored by the ACM SIGACT and SIGGRAPH
Supported by Raindrop Geomagic

The ACM Symposium on Computational Geometry in 2000 will be held at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Hong Kong. The conference will feature an applied track, a theoretical track, as well as a video review.

A workshop to celebrate Micha Sharir's 50th birthday is colocated with the conference. The workshop will take place on June 11, immediately before the symposium.
