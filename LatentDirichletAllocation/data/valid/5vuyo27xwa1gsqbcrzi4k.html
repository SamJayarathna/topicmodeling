Welcome to MobiHoc 2014

Philadelphia, PA, USA

August 11-14, 2014

ACM MobiHoc is the premier international symposium dedicated to addressing challenges emerging from wireless ad hoc networking and computing. The highly selective technical program has traditionally brought together a unique blend of researchers and practitioners thereby influencing the direction of a broad spectrum of wireless networking research.
