Conference information

The ACM Symposium on Computational Geometry in 2002 will be held at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya, Barcelona, Spain. The conference will feature an applied track, a theoretical track, as well as a video review. The Departament de Matemàtica Aplicada II will be the primary host for the conference.
