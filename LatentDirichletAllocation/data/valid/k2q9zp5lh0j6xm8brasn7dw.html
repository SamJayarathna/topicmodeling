Lisboa - September 7th-10th, 2010

Join us in sunny Lisbon, Portugal, from September 7th-10th 2010, for the 12th International Conference on Human-Computer Interaction with Mobile Devices and Services. The MobileHCI series provides a forum for academics and practitioners to discuss the challenges and potential solutions for effective interaction with mobile devices and services. It covers the design, evaluation and application of techniques and approaches for all mobile and wearable computing devices and services.
