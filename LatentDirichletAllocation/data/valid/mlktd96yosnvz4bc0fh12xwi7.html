Call for Participation!

MobiHoc 2003 is the fourth of a series of annual meetings sponsored by ACM SIGMOBILE, focusing on the latest research in the rapidly growing area of mobile ad hoc networking and computing. The first MobiHoc was held in 2000 in Boston, Massachusetts, as a workshop affiliated with the MobiCom conference also sponsored by SIGMOBILE. Since then, MobiHoc has become a separate symposium, held in 2001 in Long Beach, California, in 2002 in Lausanne, Switzerland, and now in 2003 in Annapolis, Maryland. Topics of interest include all areas of mobile and wireless ad hoc networking (above the physical layer), ad hoc computing, and sensor networks.

Paper Submission

  • First read the instructions under Submission Instructions link.
  • Then, go to http://mobihoc.ee.ucla.edu/~edas/ to register and upload your paper. Do not send your papers or abstracts by email to the General Chair or to the TPC Co-Chairs. We will be unable to consider such submissions.
  • For tutorial and poster submissions, please see the Submission Instructions link.
