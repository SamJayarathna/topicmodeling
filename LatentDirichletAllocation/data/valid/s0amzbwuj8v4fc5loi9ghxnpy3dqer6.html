CHI 2003

CHI 2003 will be held in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida. See you there.

Great Minds Gathered at CHI 2002 in Minneapolis, Minnesota USA from April 20-25.

Software designers, usability engineers, user interface designers, information architects, human factors engineers, web designers, HCI researchers and educators. CHI 2002 offered a world of opportunity to:

  • Meet face-to-face with industry gurus such as Jakob Neilsen, Donald Norman, Jared Spool, Ben Shneiderman, Kevin Warwick, and more.
  • Experience new paradigms of thought on the future of user interface.
  • Take practical tutorials from leading experts and learn new skills you could apply immediately.
  • Get psyched and get concrete information about the positive affect of good usability engineering on your entire organization or simply on your web site.
  • Meet with your peers to discuss technologies that would drive human-computer interface advancement in the decades to come. Form business contacts, pursue collaborations, and develop new clients.
  • Recruit outstanding employees, from graduating students to leaders in the HCI field.

CHI 2002, where the leading edge of usability engineering, web design, human factors, and user interface took center stage.

The CHI 2002 Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems took place in Minneapolis, Minnesota, April 20-25, 2002.

The annual CHI conference is the leading international forum for the exchange of ideas and information about computer-human interaction (CHI), also known as human-computer interaction (HCI).
