SAICSIT 2011 will this year take place from 3 to 5 October 2011 in the heart of the Cape Town Waterfront, at The Pavilion Conference Centre (www.thepavilion.co.za)

REGISTRATION CLOSES ON MONDAY, 26 SEPTEMBER, 2011 AT 10:00 CAT (UTC+2)
Click here or follow the link on the menu to register.
The conference will follow on the success of recent conferences and will again start with the postgraduate symposium and workshops/tutorials on the Monday, followed by the main SAICSIT conference on the Tuesday and Wednesday.

The main conference will be a full 2-day event, including invited speakers, papers and demonstrations from our academic and research community, our industrial partners, and many more.

Hope to see you all in Cape Town in October!
