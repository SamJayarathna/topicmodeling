Welcome to KDD 13

It is a pleasure to welcome you to the 19th ACM SIGKDD Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (KDD) which will be held in Chicago during August 11-14, 2013. Please click here for the conference program. Reservations for hotel rooms can be made on this room reservation page.

The annual ACM SIGKDD conference is the premier international forum for data mining and big data researchers and practitioners from academia, industry, and government to share their ideas, research results and experiences. KDD-2013 will feature keynote presentations, oral paper presentations, poster sessions, workshops, tutorials, panels, exhibits, demonstrations, and the KDD Cup competition.

Please join us for KDD-2013 to gain new knowledge and to learn and exchange exciting new research results, leading practices, and high impact applications in big data, knowledge discovery and data mining.

Robert L. Grossman (Univ. of Chicago and Open Data Group)
