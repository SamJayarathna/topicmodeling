Twenty-Eighth Annual ACM SIGACT-SIGOPS Symposium on Principles of Distributed Computing

Calgary, Alberta, Canada

August 10-12, 2009

Co-located with
21st ACM Symposium on Parallelism in Algorithms and Architectures (SPAA 2009)

PODC is a conference that focuses on research in the theory, design, specification and implementation of distributed systems.

PODC is sponsored by ACM, SIGACT, and SIGOPS.
