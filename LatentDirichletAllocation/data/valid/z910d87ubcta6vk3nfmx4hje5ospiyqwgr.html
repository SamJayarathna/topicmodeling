MobiSys 2013

The 11th International Conference on Mobile Systems,
Applications, and Services
Taipei, Taiwan

MobiSys 2013 seeks to present innovative and significant research on the design, implementation, usage, and evaluation of mobile computing and wireless systems, applications, and services. This conference builds on the success of the previous ten MobiSys conferences. It is sponsored by ACM SIGMOBILE.

The conference will be held at Taipei, Taiwan on June 25th - 28th 2013.
