WCCCE 2014 - The 19th Western Canadian Conference on Computing Education
02 May - 03 May, 2014
Kwantlen Polytechnic University (KPU)
Richmond, British Columbia, CANADA


                                                      
The Conference

The 19th Western Canadian Conference on Computing Education, in-cooperation with the ACM Special Interest Group on Computer Science Education (SIGCSE), is a forum for discussing the development of computing programs and curricula and for sharing innovative strategies and tools for teaching and learning in fields such as computer science, computer technology, information systems and information technology. The Conference strives to bring together university and college educators, as well as industry and government representatives who have an interest in computing education. Please refer to past WCCCE conferences for more information.

WCCCE 2014 will take place on 02 May - 03 May, 2014, and is being hosted by the Department of Computer Science and Information Technology of the School of Business, Kwantlen Polytechnic University in Richmond, BC, Canada.
