MoMM2012

The 10th International Conference on Advances in Mobile Computing & Multimedia (MoMM2012) is a leading international conference for researchers and industry practitioners to share their new ideas, original research results and practical development experiences from all mobile computing and multimedia related areas.

MoMM2012 is endorsed by the International Organization for Information Integration and Web-based Applications & Services (@WAS) and will be held from 3-5 December 2012, in Bali, Indonesia in conjunction with the 14th International Conference on Information Integration and Web-based Applications & Services (iiWAS2012).

All accepted MoMM2012 papers will be published by ACM International Conference Proceedings Series (ISBN: 978-1-4503-1307-0)
