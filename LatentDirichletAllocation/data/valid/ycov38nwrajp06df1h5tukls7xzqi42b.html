By hosting the 11th ESEC and the 15th Sigsoft FSE, ESEC/FSE 2007 will celebrate a decade since these two important events merged for the first time. As is now tradition, ESEC/FSE 2007 will provide a lively, truly international, forum for discussing innovative results and experiences in Software Engineering research and practice. We welcome informal contributions, ideas and support to help us make ESEC/FSE 2007 an unforgettable event. ESEC/FSE 2007 will be held in Dubrovnik, the jewel town in the beautiful Croatia region. Don't miss it!
