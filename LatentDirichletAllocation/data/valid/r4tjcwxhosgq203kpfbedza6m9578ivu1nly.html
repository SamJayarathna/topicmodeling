ITiCSE 2010, the 15th Annual Conference on Innovation and Technology in Computer Science Education will be held at Bilkent Hotel and Conference Center, in Ankara, Turkey and hosted by Bilkent University. The conference is sponsored by the ACM Special Interest Group on Computer Science Education (SIGCSE). The program of the conference will consist of keynote lectures, paper sessions, working groups, exhibits, panels, posters, courseware demonstrations, tips and techniques.
