ACM Symposium on Information, Computer and Communications Security (ASIACCS'08)

To build on the success of ACM Conference on Computer and Communications Security (CCS) and ACM Transactions on Information and System Security (TISSEC), the ACM Special Interest Group on Security, Audit, and Control (SIGSAC) formally established the annual ACM Symposium on InformAtion, Computer and Communications Security (ASIACCS) in 2005. The inaugural conference ASIACCS'06 was successfully held March 21-24, 2006 in Taipei, Taiwan and ASIACCS'07 was successfully held March 20-22, 2007, in Singapore. The third conference ASIACCS'08 will be held March 2008, Tokyo, Japan.
