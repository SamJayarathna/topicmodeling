The IEEE/ACM ASE conference series is one of the world’s premier Software Engineering conferences. Each year, it brings together researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to discuss foundations, techniques and tools for automating the analysis, design, implementation, testing, and maintenance of large software systems. ASE 2012 will include technical papers, experience papers, invited keynotes, tutorials, workshops, tool demonstrations and a doctoral symposium.

The ASE 2012 conference will be held from September 3-7, 2012 in Essen, Germany.
