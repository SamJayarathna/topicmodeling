GRAPHITE 2006

[Introduction :: Best Paper Award :: Next Graphite (2007) :: Important Dates ::Latest News :: Mailing List ]

GRAPHITE 2006, 29 November – 2 December 2006
Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Sponsored by ACM SIGGRAPH, GRAPHITE 2006 is the 4th International Conference on Computer Graphics and Interactive Techniques in Australasia and the Southeast Asia, an annual event conducted in the Australasia-SEA region. Following the success of the first Conference in Melbourne, Australia, the second in Singapore and the third in Dunedin, New Zealand, this year’s conference will be conducted at Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre, KLCC in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Universiti Teknologi Malaysia, UTM is the main organiser of the conference.

With the aim of bringing together and fostering the development of the computer graphics community in the region, this conference offers exciting and information-rich events like:

  • Keynotes by distinguish speakers
  • Technical presentation
  • Tutorials
  • Electronic theatre and digital art gallery
  • Forum
  • Exhibition
