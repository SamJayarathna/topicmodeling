Skin lightening products popularly known as whiteners, creams, fade or skin brighteners function in reducing melanin a skin pigment. Skin lightening products are mostly used to cure skin conditions like acne, age spots and discoloration. It is also applied in lightening dark skin. Skin lightening products often come with tremendous risks hence be sure to read http://skinlighteningcreamworld.com/ for important information about various skin lightening products.

The amount of melanin on the skin determines to a great extent the color of the skin hence you will find more melanin in people with dark skin. It is a matter of genetic makeup since melanin is basically produced by melanocytes, specialized cells found in the body.

Melanin production in the body is affected by various factors such as prolonged exposure of the skin to the sun, certain chemicals, hormones as well as skin damage.

What to do?

Use skin lightening products that are medically approved to lighten your skin. Skin lightening products basically contain active or a combination of ingredients that help to reduce the production of melanin. You will find products made of natural ingredients and other products made of artificial ingredients. Regardless of the ingredient, make sure that you pick a product that is effective in lightening your skin as well as safe as not to cause any side effects or allergic reactions. Those with sensitive skin are always recommended to consult a dermatologist first before using any skin care products.

What not to do?

Bleaching is a well-known and very popular cosmetic treatment for skin discoloration. There are many over the counter bleaching prescription which one can use to bleach their skin. You will find individuals with dark skin applying bleaching agents all over their skin in order to change their complexion. As much as it is very effective it is also very dangerous.

The active ingredient used in making most skin bleaching products is mercury. For your information mercury is toxic and poisonous.  Mercury can lead to neurological, psychiatric and kidney problems. Pregnant women are restricted from using skin lightening products with mercury since the side effects can be passed to the unborn child. This is the reason why you will find the use of skin lightening products containing mercury being banned in most countries.

In summary, there arenumerous skin lightening products in the market from different manufacturers or brands. The prices vary depending on the vendor, brand and other factors. Make sure you choose the best at a good price.

Wrinkle Creams That Provide The Best Cosmetic Results

Posted on 5 July, 2013 in Health and Fitness

It is common for everyone to get wrinkles especially at the onset of old age. As one grows mainly in the youthful stage, cell division is very active however as aging approaches the cells start to divide slowly as the dermis (inner skin layer) starts to thicken. This interferes with normal skin formation, structure and stretchiness. The skin also starts to lose moisture holding ability, healing ability and the oil content of skin reduces. All these factors contribute to the formation of wrinkles.

Facial muscle contraction is a normal body process that is known to increase the formation and expression of wrinkles on the face. These include activities like frowning, smiling and squinting. Excessive exposure of the skin to direct sunlight is also known to trigger wrinkle formation. Individuals are recommended to wear sunscreen or protective clothing whenever going out door or rather limit outdoor activities to prevent wrinkle formation.

Collagen is the key component of the skin structure and formation. Habits like smoking interfere with production of collagen which in turn results to wrinkle formation. This is why it is always recommended for one to cease smoking.

Wrinkle treatment solutions

There are many wrinkle treatment methods that have been proposed and are being used by different people across the globe. Make sure you have a look at various wrinkle cream reviews online to find out some of the alternatives available for you.

Wrinkle treatment ranges from traditional methods to modern medical techniques. Traditional techniques mainly involve the use of herbs however there is no conclusive evidence that they get rid of wrinkles completely.

Over the counter prescriptions like use of wrinkle creams and lotions are known to be very effective in getting rid of wrinkles. Wrinkle creams basically help to repair and nourish the skin reviving the young and appealing look.

Other wrinkle treatments involve scrapping the layers of the skin or dissolving the skin using chemical peels. These are traditional techniques that are still in use in the modern society. Surgery is medical treatment for removing wrinkles. Surgery procedures involve laser treatment that aims at approaching treatment from the deeper layers of the skin. Other treatments involve stimulating the dermis to produce more collagen.

With all being said, use of wrinkle creams remains to be the easiest, effective and safest way of removing wrinkles. Since there are many wrinkle creams in the market today, make sure you read wrinkle cream reviews available online for you to find the best.
