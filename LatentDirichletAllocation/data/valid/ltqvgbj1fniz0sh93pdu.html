The 12th International Conference on Information Integration and Web-based Applications & Services (iiWAS2010) is a leading international conference for researchers and industry practitioners to share their new ideas, original research results and practical development experiences from all information integration and web-based applications & services related areas.

iiWAS2010 is endorsed by the International Organization for Information Integration and Web-based Applications & Services (@WAS), organized by The University of Paris 8 and The University of Cergy-Pontoise, Paris (France) and will be held from 8-10 November 2010, in Paris, France in conjunction with the 8th International Conference on Advances in Mobile Computing And Multimedia (MoMM2010) and the International Conference on Ambient Systems, Networks and Technologies (ANT-2010)
