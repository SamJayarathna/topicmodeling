Overview

The Tenth ACM Workshop on Hot Topics in Networks (HotNets-X) will bring together people with interest in computer networks to engage in a lively debate on theory and practice of networking. Continuing the HotNets tradition, the workshop will provide a venue for presenting and discussing innovative ideas that have the potential to significantly influence the community. The goal is to promote community-wide discussions of those ideas.

The HotNets workshop will be held at the MIT Faculty Club (50 Memorial Drive, Bldg. E52, 6th Floor, Cambridge, MA; directions from workshop hotels).
