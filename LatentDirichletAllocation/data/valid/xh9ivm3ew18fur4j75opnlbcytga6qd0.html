The International Symposium on Software Testing and Analysis (ISSTA)
London, United Kingdom, July 9th - 12th, 2007.

Over one hundred researchers and practitioners attended ISSTA 2007 in London, from July 9 - 12, 2007. We are thankful to the keynote speakers, authors, volunteers, and participants for helping make this a successful event.

The ISSTA 2007 proceedings are available at the ACM portal, and you can directly retrieve the keynote presentations of Dawson Engler and Tim Trew.
