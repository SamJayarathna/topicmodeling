Welcome to SYSTOR, starting Sunday June 30 at the IBM Research - Haifa auditorium.

Participation is FREE, but registration is required - click here to register.

On Sunday, June 30 at 10.30am join us for the opening keynote from Brian Levine (University of Massachusetts Amherst) on "Fighting Internet-based Sexual Exploitation Crimes Against Children."

You are invited to the SYSTOR beer garden and poster session on Sunday at 5pm. Come help us select the SYSTOR best poster.

On Monday, July 1 at 9:00am, please join us for Brian Cooper's (Google) keynote about "Spanner: Google's Globally-Distributed Database."

On Tuesday, July 2, join us for Thomas Gross's (ETH Zurich) keynote on "Big Data -- Little Software?"
