9th International Conference on Testbeds and Research Infrastructures for the Development of Networks and Communities

Highlights:

  • Please kindly note: The address of the conference venue is Guangwu Hotel (广州莱福广武酒店). The web of Guangwu Hotel: http://www.huilv.com/EN/Home.asp?id=13765).
  • The deadline is extended to Feb. 15th, 2014.

The 9th International Conference on Testbeds and Research Infrastructures for the Development of Networks and Communities will be held in Guangzhou, China, 5-7 of May 2014, with the vision to bring together technical experts and researchers from academia, industry and government all around the world to discuss the convergence of advanced networking and cloud computing.

Tridentcom 2014 will serve as a forum where researchers, vendors, providers and users will exchange ideas on past experience, requirements, needs and visions for establishing the convergence of advanced networking and cloud computing. The focus of the conference is on activities related to experimentation such as testing, verification, deployment, integration, management and federation of such facilities as well as experimentation oriented research based on implementation of novel schemes on research testbeds.

Apart from regular paper presentations, Tridentcom 2014 will feature a rich variety of other activities such as tutorials, workshops, panels and demos in order to enhance the visibility of innovative mechanisms and practices in the field as well as to strength the interaction and the communication in the community of research testbeds.

All accepted papers will be published in Springer's LNICST series, will appear in the SpringerLink, one of the largest digital libraries online that covers a variety of scientific disciplines and indexed by EI Compendex.

We welcome you to China and Tridentcom 2014.

Victor C. M. Leung, General Chair
