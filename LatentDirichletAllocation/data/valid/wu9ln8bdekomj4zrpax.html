St Andrews, UK, Oct 6-9, 2013

 

St Andrews, UK, Oct 6-9, 2013

 
 

Welcome

ITS 2013 is over, ITS 2014 will be held in Dresden, Germany!

The ACM Interactive Tabletops and Surfaces 2013 Conference (ITS) is a premiere venue for research in the design and use of new and emerging tabletop and interactive surface technologies. We are an emerging community which embraces a wide variety of areas including innovations in ITS interaction design, hardware, software, and projects that build understanding of ITS technology design considerations and applications.

Complete Submissions to ITS 2013 this year: 123 (34 notes). Of these, 26 were application papers. This is up 18% from last year. A big thanks to the program committee who are already working hard dealing with these submissions!
