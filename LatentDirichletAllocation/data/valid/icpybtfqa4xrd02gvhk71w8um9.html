VLDB is a premier international conference on database technology, organized every year by the VLDB Endowment. VLDB 2001 will be held in Roma, one of the most important historical cities in the world, with an uncountable number of interesting monuments. Proceedings from earlier VLDB Conferences are distributed by Morgan Kaufmann. The VLDB Endowment also publishes the VLDB Journal through Springer.
