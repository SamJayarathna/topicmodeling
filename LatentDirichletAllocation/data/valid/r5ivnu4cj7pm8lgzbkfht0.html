About iiWAS2009

The 11th International Conference on Information Integration and Web-based Applications & Services (iiWAS2009) is a leading international conference for researchers and industry practitioners to share their new ideas, original research results and practical development experiences from all information integration and web-based applications & services related areas.

iiWAS2009 is endorsed by the International Organization for Information Integration and Web-based Applications & Services (@WAS), organized by Asia e University – Kuala Lumpur (Malaysia) and will be held from 14-16 December 2009, in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia in conjunction with the 7th International Conference on Advances in Mobile Computing And Multimedia (MoMM2009).

Highlights @ iiWAS2009

  • Proceedings will be published by the ACM Digital Library
  • Five exciting keynote speeches by:
       1. Gabriele Kotsis (Vice Rector, Johannes Kepler University Linz, Austria)
       2. Won Kim (Pioneer of Object-Oriented Databases)
       3. Stephan Olariu (Associate Editor IEEE TPDS)
       4. Frank Dignum (Authority in Computer Science)
       5. Dzaharudin Mansor (Microsoft Malaysia)
  • Four tutorials on Model Driven Engineering, High Performance Databases, Ontology Activity Recognition, and Web 2.0 and Beyond
  • Eight in conjunction workshops
  • A panel on Service Computing in the Clouds
  • Web application competition
  • Special issues in six international journals.

Emerald Group Publishing the world's leading publisher of management research will give all iiWAS2009 participants an open access to IJWIS for 14 – 20th December.
