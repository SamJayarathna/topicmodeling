Beauty patch developed by VTT generates its power from sugar and air

A disposable beauty patch using microampere current to enhance absorption of skin care products will offer future consumers an opportunity to perform galvanic skin treatments at home. At the moment, this can only be done by beauticians using special devices. The product idea developed at VTT Technical Research Centre of Finland can also be applied to medicinal products.Read more...
