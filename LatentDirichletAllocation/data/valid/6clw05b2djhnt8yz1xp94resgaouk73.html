The 2014 Internet Measurement Conference (IMC) is a three-day event focusing on Internet measurement and analysis. The conference is sponsored jointly by ACM SIGCOMM and ACM SIGMETRICS. IMC 2014 is the 14th in a series of highly successful Internet Measurement Workshops and Conferences.

The ACM IMC 2014 conference will be held in Vancouver, BC, Canada on November 5-7, 2014.

Conference registration is now open! The early registration deadline is October 1, and the hotel reservation deadline is October 7. Here is a Facebook group to facilitate room-sharing arrangements for graduate students, or other discussions of conference travel logistics.

The full technical program is now available online! There is also an open Call for Presentations for the Community Feedback session at the conference.
