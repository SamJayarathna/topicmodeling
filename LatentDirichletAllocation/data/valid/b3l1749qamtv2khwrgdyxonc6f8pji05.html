Welcome to PPPJ 2011

The 9th International Conference on the Principles and Practice of Programming in Java will bring together researchers, teachers, practitioners and programmers who study or work with the Java language or its virtual machine. The conference provides an opportunity to discuss principles, best practices and new ideas in a friendly and fertile academic environment.

Previous PPPJs have been held in Vienna, Calgary, Modena, and Lisbon. For more information on past PPPJs, visit PPPJ.info.
