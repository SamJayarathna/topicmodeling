Welcome to MobiHoc 2013

ACM MobiHoc is the premier international symposium dedicated to addressing challenges emerging from wireless ad hoc networking and computing.

The highly selective technical program has traditionally brought together a unique blend of researchers and practitioners thereby influencing the direction of a broad spectrum of wireless networking research.
