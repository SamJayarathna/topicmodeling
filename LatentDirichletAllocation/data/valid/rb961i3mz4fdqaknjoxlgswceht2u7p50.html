BELIV 2014 announced
The BELIV workshop series is a bi-annual event focusing on the challenges of evaluation in visualization. BELIV 2014 will be the fifth of the BELIV workshop series and will be held Nov 10, 2014, as a one-day workshop at IEEE VIS 2014 in Paris. All registered attendees of VisWeek will be able to attend the workshop.
