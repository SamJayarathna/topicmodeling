Post MUM 2012 Information

The 12th International Conference on Mobile and Ubiquitous Multimedia (MUM 2013) will be held in Luleå, Sweden, December 9-12, 2013. It is organized by the Pervasive and Mobile Computing Group, of the Department of Computer Science, Electrical and Space Engineering of Luleå University of Technology.

The best long paper award of MUM 2012 has been given to Toolkit Support for Projected Ubiquitous Displays by John Hardy and Jason Alexander (Lancaster University, UK). The best short paper award of MUM 2012 has been given to Dynamic Tiling Display: Building an Interactive Display Surface using Multiple Mobile Devices by Ming Li and Leif Kobbelt (RWTH Aachen, Germany).

All papers, posters and demos are available in the ACM Digital Library.



Welcome to MUM 2012

MUM 2012 is a leading annual international conference, which provides a forum for presenting the latest research results on mobile and ubiquitous multimedia. The conference brings together experts from both academia and industry for a fruitful exchange of ideas and discussion on future challenges, in a comfortable and effective single track conference format.

The 11th International Conference on Mobile and Ubiquitous Multimedia (MUM 2012) will be held in Ulm, Germany, December 4-6, 2012. It is organized by the Institute of Media Informatics of the Ulm University in-cooperation with ACM SIGCHI.

In addition to the peer-reviewed accepted papers, the conference program will include keynote presentations (Dieter Schmalstieg and Nigel Davies), posters, demos and an industry track. The technical program will be complemented by several social events to facilitate informal discussions and networking among the conference attendees and invited guests.

Best Paper Award and Best Student Paper Award will be presented for outstanding contributions.

The participants of MUM 2012 will be able to enjoy the well-known Ulm Christmas Market ("Weihnachtsmarkt"), the Christmas illuminations and German as well as international Christmas presents, food and entertainment.

We invite you to begin planning your submissions and participation for MUM 2012!
