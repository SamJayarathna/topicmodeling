The 2011 Internet Measurement Conference is a three day event focusing on Internet measurement and analysis, following on the successes of the ten previous Internet Measurement Workshops and Conferences.

IMPORTANT NOTE: For IMC attendees (or an accompanying person) to be able to enter the German parliament (we will arrange for such tours in the evening of Thursday 3rd of November), it is strictly necessary that they register for the conference (and provide the name and date of birth of the accompanying person). The strict security measures associated with a visit of the parliament require that we provide name and birth of date for every person who plans to join the tour by October 9. Attendees registering after October 9 will not be able to join the IMC-arranged tour of the German Parliament.
