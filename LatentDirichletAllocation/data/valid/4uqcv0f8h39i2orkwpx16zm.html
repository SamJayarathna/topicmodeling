Baltimore, Maryland, July 12–17, 2015

 

International Symposium on Software Testing and Analysis

ISSTA is the leading research symposium on software testing and analysis, bringing together academics, industrial researchers, and practitioners to exchange new ideas, problems, and experience on how to analyze and test software systems. ISSTA 2015 will be held in Holiday Inn Inner Harbor, Baltimore, Maryland on July 12-17, 2015.
