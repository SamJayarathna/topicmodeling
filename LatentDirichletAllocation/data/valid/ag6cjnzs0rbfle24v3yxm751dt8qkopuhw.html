Sponsored by ACM SIGOPS in cooperation with INRIA


This conference brings together developers and researchers from university, industry and government to advance the science and technology in operating systems. Its three-day technical program includes 23 papers, invited talks and a Work-in-Progress and Poster session.

The conference will open on Sunday, October 5 at 6:00 pm and will close on Wednesday, October 8 at 2:30 pm.
