Welcome

Welcome to the home of the 2012 ACM SIGMOD/PODS Conference, to be held in the city of Scottsdale, Arizona, USA on May 20-24, 2012.

The annual ACM SIGMOD/PODS conference is a leading international forum for database researchers, practitioners, developers, and users to explore cutting-edge ideas and results, and to exchange techniques, tools, and experiences.

Scottsdale, Arizona promises stunning desert vistas and a breathtaking setting for this year's conference. Adjacent to Phoenix, the fifth most populated city in the United States, many attractions, parks, and recreational areas — including the Grand Canyon — are within a short distance. The conference will be held at the Hyatt Regency Scottsdale Resort and Spa at Gainey Ranch. This 27-acre hotel resort, minutes from a host of activities and miles from the ordinary, promises an exciting venue for this year's conference.
