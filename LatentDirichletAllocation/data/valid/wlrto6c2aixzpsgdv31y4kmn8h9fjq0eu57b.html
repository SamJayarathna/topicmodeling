Following the success of the past conferences in this series (Lake District/UK, Palisades/NY, and Heidelberg/Germany), and building upon the success of past COOTS conferences, the 4th Middleware has been the premier conference on middleware research and technology in 2003. The scope of the conference was the design, implementation, deployment, and evaluation of distributed systems platforms and architectures for future computing environments.

Highlights of the conference included:

  • a high-quality technical program
  • work-in-progress presentations
  • outstanding tutorials
  • invited keynote speaker
  • poster presentations
  • advanced workshops
