Welcome

Welcome to the home page of the 2010 International Conference on Software Testing and Analysis (ISSTA 2010).

ISSTA is the leading research conference in software testing and analysis, bringing together academics, industrial researchers, and practitioners to exchange new ideas, problems, and experience on how to analyze and test software systems. The ISSTA program will include technical papers, keynotes, workshops, and a doctoral symposium.

ISSTA 2010 will be held in Trento, Italy. Trento is a small city in the middle of the Alps whose center has a strong Renaissance mark, including several buildings with frescos. Also notable in Trento are Castello del Buonconsiglio and several medieval churches, among which the Duomo. The surroundings of Trento offer beautiful naturalistic tracks on top of mountains (e.g., Monte Bondone), around the numerous lakes, or nearby ancient castles. More information about Trento is available at: http://www.apt.trento.it.

We look forward to seeing you in Trento in July!
