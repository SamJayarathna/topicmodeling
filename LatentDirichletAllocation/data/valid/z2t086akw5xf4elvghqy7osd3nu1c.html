See you in iiWAS2012/MoMM2012, Bali, Indonesia, 3-5 December 2012


The 9th International Conference on Advances in Mobile Computing & Multimedia (MoMM-2011) is a leading international conference for researchers and industry practitioners to share their new ideas, original research results and practical development experiences from all mobile computing and multimedia related areas.

MoMM-2011 is endorsed by the International Organization for Information Integration and Web-based Applications & Services (@WAS), organized by International University (Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam) and will be held from 5-7 December 2011, in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam in conjunction with the 13th International Conference on Information Integration and Web-based Applications & Services (iiWAS2011).
