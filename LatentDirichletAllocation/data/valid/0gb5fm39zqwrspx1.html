Welcome to the AAMAS-2010 webpage!

AAMAS-2010, the 9th International Conference on Autonomous Agents and Multiagent Systems will take place at the Sheraton Centre Toronto Hotel in downtown Toronto Canada, on May 10-14 2010. AAMAS is the premier scientific conference for research on autonomous agents and multiagent systems.

AAMAS-2010 is collocated with KR, NMR, ICAPS, FOIS, (all in Toronto) and DL 2010 (in Waterloo, Ontario, Canada) .

Important Dates

  • Electronic Abstract Submission: October 8, 2009 by 11:59pm HST (Hawaii-Aleutian Standard Time)
  • Full Paper Submission: October 13, 2009 by 11:59pm HST (Hawaii-Aleutian Standard Time)
  • Rebuttal phase: November 29, 2009 00:01am to December 1, 2009 11:59pm HST (Hawaii-Aleutian Standard Time)
  • Author Notification: December 18, 2009
  • Conference: May 10-14, 2010
