      Welcome to IHI 2012

       
      Welcome to the web site of the 2012 ACM SIGHIT International Health Informatics Symposium (IHI 2012), the flagship conference of the newly formed ACM Special Interest Group on Health Informatics (SIGHIT)! The annual ACM IHI conference is the premier international forum concerned with the application of computer science principles, information science principles, information technology, and communication technology to address problems in healthcare, public health, everyday wellness as well as the related social and ethical issues.
       
      The IHI 2012 will be a forum for demo and paper contributions from researchers, practitioners, developers, and users to explore and disseminate cutting-edge ideas and results, and to exchange techniques, tools, and experiences.
