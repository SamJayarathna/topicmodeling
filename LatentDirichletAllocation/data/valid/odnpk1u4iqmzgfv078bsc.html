Twenty-Fourth Annual ACM SIGACT-SIGOPS Symposium on
Principles of Distributed Computing
(PODC 2005)

July 17-20, 2005, Las Vegas, Nevada, USA

PODC is a conference that focuses on research in the theory, design, specification and implementation of distributed systems. PODC is sponsored by ACM SIGACT and SIGOPS.

This year, the conference is co-located with the ACM Symposium on Parallelism in Algorithms and Architectures (SPAA 2005).
