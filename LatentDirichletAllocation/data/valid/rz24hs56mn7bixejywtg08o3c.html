Welcome to MobiHoc 2011

MobiHoc 2011, the Twelfth ACM International Symposium on Mobile Ad Hoc Networking and Computing, is the eleventh of a series of annual meetings sponsored by ACM SIGMOBILE, focusing on the latest research in the rapidly growing area of mobile ad hoc networking and computing. This symposium will be held in Paris, France, May 16 - 19, 2011. It will bring together researchers and practitioners from a broad spectrum of wireless networking research to present the most up-to-date results and achievements in the field. The symposium will include a highly selective technical program, a distinguished keynote address, and panels.


 MobiHoc 2011 is sponsored by ACM SIGMOBILE
