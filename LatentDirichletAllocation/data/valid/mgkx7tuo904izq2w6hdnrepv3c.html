SIGCSE

Schedule of Conferences and Symposia



SIGCSE, the ACM Special Interest Group on Computer Science Education, sponsors 2-3 events annually:

  • an annual Symposium, held in February or March in the United States,
  • an annual summer conference on Innovation and Technology in Computer Science Education, held in Europe, and
  • a periodic conference, held in December in Australia.

Notes:

  • This listing identifies anticipated locations, dates, and leadership. Since all ACM-related conferences must follow a formal approval process to be officially listed on an ACM Web site, some of the future conferences listed here must be regarded as unofficial. Typically all SIGCSE conferences submit this required paperwork between 6 months and 1 year before the actual event.
  • In the following listing, links for the various conferences and symposia are added as they become available.
