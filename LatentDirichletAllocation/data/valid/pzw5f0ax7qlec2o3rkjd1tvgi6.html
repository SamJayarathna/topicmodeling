SIGIR is the major international forum for the presentation of new research results and the demonstration of new systems and techniques in the broad field of information retrieval.

The 27th Annual International ACM SIGIR Conference was held at The University of Sheffield, UK, from July 25 to July 29, 2004.

News

12 Oct 04 

The conference is over! With around 430 attending the conference, tutorials and workshops, it was one of the best attended SIGIRs ever. Thanks to everyone who made the conference possible.

A few photos have been added. Conference proceedings are on the ACM Portal. Workshop and docutoral consortium reports are in the December 2004 edition of SIGIR Forum.
