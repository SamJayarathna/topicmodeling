MobiHoc 2005, the Sixth ACM International Symposium on Mobile Ad Hoc Networking and Computing, is the sixth of a series of annual meetings sponsored by ACM SIGMOBILE, focusing on the latest research in the rapidly growing area of mobile ad hoc networking and computing. MobiHoc 2005 will be held in Urbana-Champaign, IL,USA, May 25-28, 2005.

The first MobiHoc was held in 2000 in Boston, Massachusetts, as a workshop affiliated with the MobiCom conference also sponsored by SIGMOBILE. Since then, MobiHoc has become a separate symposium, held in 2001 at Long Beach, California, in 2002 at Lausanne, Switzerland, in 2003 at Annapolis, Maryland, in 2004 at Tokyo, Japan, and now in 2005 at Urbana-Champaign, Illinois.

Due to the growing interest in the field, MobiHoc 2005 will have an expanded format that will include a highly selective dual-track technical program for the first time, as well as hands-on research demonstrations, keynote, panel, and tutorials.
