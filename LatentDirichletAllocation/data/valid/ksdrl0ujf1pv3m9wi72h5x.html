MobiSys 2014

The 12th International Conference on Mobile Systems,
Applications, and Services
Bretton Woods, NH, USA

MobiSys 2014 seeks to present innovative and significant research on the design, implementation, usage, and evaluation of mobile computing and wireless systems, applications, and services. This conference builds on the success of the previous eleven MobiSys conferences. It is sponsored by ACM SIGMOBILE.
