LNCS Post-Proceedings of Six Workshops on Behaviour Modelling. Foundations and Applications

The workshop on Behaviour Modelling - Foundations and Applications attracts attention to the role of behaviour modelling. In the domain of Business Process Management behaviour models have been successfully used for over 20 years. The growing importance of service-orientation and business process management increases the role of behaviour modelling for all activities of the system life cycle. The behaviour modelling can contribute to quality of applications in a huge domain from embedded systems, to logistic systems, case management solutions and systems of communicating services.

In 2014, we propose the 6th edition of a BM-FA workshop, which has the goal to focus on possibilities and advantages of the practical use of behaviour models for MDA. In order to show the value created by behaviour models, we would like to focus this years workshop on contributions that show industrial success stories and on experience reports, which identify the pressing issues in the practical use of behaviour model in industrial-scale projects.
Therefore, we announce a special format for participation of industrial participants. We shall accept industrial participants who submit a one page abstract about the application of behaviour models and will present their work at the workshop.

For the participants from academia, we guarantee an interesting discussion of the industrial experience and its theoretic basis. Our Program Committee is ready to give a valuable feedback and choose the quality papers for publication.
