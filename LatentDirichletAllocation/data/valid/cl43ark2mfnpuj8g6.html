The International Symposium on Low Power Electronics and Design (ISLPED) is the premier forum for presentation of recent advances in all aspects of low power design and technologies, ranging from process and circuit technologies, to simulation and synthesis tools, to system level design and optimization.

News


  1. (Sep/11/2014) After the successful past editions in Asia and in the USA, ISLPED gets back to Europe in 2015. The chosen location is Rome, Italy, from July 22nd to July 24th. The conference venue will be Sapienza University at San Pietro in Vincoli Cloister, a five-century-old building in the middle of the historical center of the "eternal city", at three-minute walking distance from the Colosseum.
  2. (Sep/12/2014) Starting from this year ISLPED is on Twitter. Follow and stay tuned to receive info and real-time update: Follow @islped
  3. (Sep/12/2014) General Chairs: Luca Benini (Univ. of Bologna / ETH Zurich), Renu Mehra (Synopsys) and Mauro Olivieri (Sapienza Univ. of Rome).

Venue


  • Sapienza University of Rome, Faculty of Engineering at the Cloister of San Pietro in Vincoli (Saint Peter in Chains), Via Eudossiana 18, Rome, Italy. Google map link .



    The Cloister is a historical building originally part of the Monastery associated to the neighboring Church of San Pietro in Vincoli, that hosts Michelangelo's statue of Moses. Wikipedia link

Topics and submission format


The list of specific topics addressed by the 2015 event will be available later at this website.

Submissions should be full-length papers of up to 6 pages (double-column, IEEE Conference Proceedings format, available here, including all illustrations, tables, references and abstract. Papers exceeding the six-page limit will not be reviewed. Submission must be anonymous: papers identifying the authors and/or with explicit references to their prior work will be automatically rejected. Electronic submission in pdf format only via the web is required. Detailed call of Papers as well as information on electronic submission will be available later at this website. Invited Talk, Panel, and Tutorial call for proposals will be available later at this website.
