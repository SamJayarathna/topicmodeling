EC'12 will be held June 4-8, 2012, in Valencia, Spain, co-located with the AAMAS 2012 Conference

Since 1999 the ACM Special Interest Group on Electronic Commerce (SIGecom) has sponsored the leading scientific conference on advances in theory, systems, and applications for electronic commerce. The Thirteenth ACM Conference on Electronic Commerce (EC'12) will feature invited speakers, paper presentations, workshops, and tutorials covering all areas of electronic commerce. The natural focus of the conference is on computer science issues, but the conference is interdisciplinary in nature.

The conference will be held from June 4 through June 6, 2012, in Valencia, Spain. Tutorials and Workshops will be held on June 7 and June 8.

Co-Located Conferences and Events
EC'12 is co-located with the AAMAS 2012 conference.

Anyone registered for an EC activity (conference or tutorial/workshop) has access to the AAMAS main conference sessions on the same day with the EC badge. Note that this does not extend to AAMAS workshops and tutorials. Attendees registered for AAMAS activities similarly have access to the EC main conference sessions on the same day with their AAMAS badge.

Conference registration:
The main conference registration will take place in the School of Agricultural Engineering and Environment, Polytechnical University of Valencia (link to http://www.sigecom.org/ec12/venue.html). Please allow 45 minutes to reach the campus from the hotel on the first day.

A registration desk will be set up in the Holiday Inn Hotel on Sunday, June 3rd, from 6:00-9:00 pm.

We will provide all registrants to the main conference with a public transport card (link to http://www.emtvalencia.es/ciudadano/index.php) good for 10 rides including transfers on the Valencia public transit system.
